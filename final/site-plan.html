Site Name

"El Brasa" Chicken & Grill

Reason: This name clearly indicates the type of food (Grilled Chicken/Pollos a la Brasa) which is very popular in Bolivia. It is short, easy to remember, and sounds appetizing.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an online menu for customers in La Paz. It will allow users to see the daily specials, check the prices of combos, and contact the restaurant to place an order via WhatsApp or phone call.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A father wants to buy dinner for his family. He asks: "What family combos do you have and how much do they cost?"
  • Scenario 2: A student wants a quick lunch. They ask: "Do you sell quarter chickens and where is the restaurant located?"
